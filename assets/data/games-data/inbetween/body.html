INBETWEEN is a liminal-space horror game and experience inspired by the Backrooms, liminal photography, Trevor Henderson based beings, and slow-burn dread.

We are currently in pre-production. Full development has not started yet and INBETWEEN is scheduled to start development by 4/01/2026.
